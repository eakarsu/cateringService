To test the new features, first start the application:

  cd /Users/erolakarsu/projects/cateringService
  ./start.sh

  Then open http://localhost:3000 in your browser.

  Login

  Use the Demo Login buttons on the login page:
  - Click "Use Demo Login (Admin)" or any account card to auto-fill credentials
  - Click "Sign In"

  Quick Test Path

  1. Login → Click "Use Demo Login (Admin)" → Sign In
  2. Guests → Click "Guests" in sidebar → Click "New Guest" → Fill form with dietary options
  3. Suppliers → Click "Suppliers" → Click "New Supplier" → Add vendor info
  4. Cost Estimator → Enter guest count, select package, adjust profit margin
  5. Kitchen Scaling → Go to Kitchen → Click "Scaling Calculator" tab → Select recipe and guest count →
  Click "Scale Recipe"
